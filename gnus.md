- `q`: Quit Gnus
- `m`: Compose new message; `C-c C-c` to send, `C-c C-d` to save to drafts.
- `r`: Reply to email without quoting.
- `R`: Reply to email with quote.
- `C-u M-g`: Display all messages, even read ones.
- `C-u g`: Display source.

# Drafts

In Drafts group:

- `D e`: Edit a draft
